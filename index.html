Flaptus

The epic flappy cactus game!

Gameplay

Welcome to Flaptus, where you hop around the world of a singular cactus and lots of rock columns!

How

Click or press space to jump through the rocks. If you touch the rocks or the floor, you lose!

As you get more points, the columns start coming at you faster and faster!

Leaderboard

Compare your score to others with a leaderboard and try to get to the top!
